Government Engineering College Siwan

GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE(GEC), Siwan is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. It will start its first academic session 2019 from campus of Government Polytechnic Siwan, Bihar. The new engineering college will have three engineering branches Civil, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, with an intake of 60, 60 and 120 per branch respectively and 240 students in total.
