Bakhtiyarpur College of Engineering

Bakhtiyarpur College Of Engineering, Patna was established in the year 2016. The institute is also known as BCE Patna. Bakhtiyarpur College Of Engineering, Patna stand amongst the six newly established Government Engineering College by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. BCE Patna was earlier functioning in the old campus of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Patna at Patliputra Colony and later on in the year 2020 on February 2, it shifted to its newly built permanent campus at Bakhtiyarpur. Bakhtiyarpur College Of Engineering, Patna is affiliated to Bihar Engineering University, Patna and is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of Human Resource.
