Government Engineering College Lakhisarai

Government Engineering College, Lakhisarai is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. The new engineering college will have three engineering branches Civil, Mechanical, Electrical with an intake of 120,60,60 and 240 students in total.
