Government Engineering College Nawada

GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE(GEC),Nawada is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. It will start its first academic session 2019 from campus of Govt.Polytechnic Nawada, Bihar. The new engineering college will have four engineering branches Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Electronics & Communication with an intake of 60 per branch and 240 students in total.
