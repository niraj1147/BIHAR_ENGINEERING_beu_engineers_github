Government Engineering College Munger

Government Engineering College Munger is the premier institute of Bihar under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of Bihar. It is approved and recognized by AICTE and is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna. College is offering B.Tech courses in Civil Engineering with an intake of 120, Mechanical Engineering with an intake of 60, and Electrical Engineering with an intake of 60. Under the expert leadership and guidance of the Department of Science and Technology.
