Motihari College of Engineering

MCE Motihari was set up by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar in the year 1986 with a view of providing an efficient system of technical education in Bihar. Earlier, the institute was known as Indian College of Engineering (ICE) and was established on 24 November 1980. Later, the college was taken over by the Bihar government in 1986. The institute also caters to the research and development activities of the state of Bihar.
