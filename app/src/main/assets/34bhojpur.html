Government Engineering College Bhojpur

GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE(GCE), Bhojpur is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. It will start its first academic session 2019 from Campus of Bakhtiyarpur College of Engineering,Bakhtiyarpur, Bihar. The new engineering college will have four engineering branches Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Instrumentation Engineering with an intake of 60 per branch and 240 students in total
