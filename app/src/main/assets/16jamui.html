Government Engineering College Jamui

Government Engineering College(GEC), Jamui is a government engineering college in Jamui district of Bihar. It was established in the year 2018 under Department of Science and Technology, Bihar. It is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna and approved by All India Council for Technical Education. College have four branches in Bachelor of Technology course with annual intake of 60 students in each branch. Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering.
